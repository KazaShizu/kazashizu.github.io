ABOUT OUR TEAM

We are Group 2 from the P5 RPL (Rekayasa Perangkat Lunak) project at SMAS Pelita Raya (Pelitaraya Private Senior High School) throughout the first semester. Our chosen project revolves around the concept of a library, and our collective effort is dedicated to crafting a website that reflects this vision. Our team places a strong emphasis on not rushing through the process of creating something meaningful. Our vision and mission involve a commitment to thoughtful and thorough work, ensuring that every aspect of the website is meticulously designed and serves its purpose effectively. We believe that taking the time to understand and implement our ideas will result in a website that is not only functional but also resonates with the essence of a modern and dynamic library.


Q&A


Q: What inspired your choice of the library as the project focus?

A: Our decision to center our project around a library stems from its integral role as a hub of knowledge and learning. We believe that a well-designed website can enhance the overall library experience for both students and faculty, providing seamless access to resources and fostering a vibrant academic community.

Q: Can you share more about your team dynamics during the semester-long project?

A: Absolutely. Our team, Group 2, operates cohesively by leveraging individual strengths. Regular communication and collaborative decision-making have been key aspects of our dynamic. We hold weekly meetings to discuss progress, address challenges, and brainstorm innovative ideas. This approach ensures that everyone is on the same page, contributing to the project's success.

Q: How do you envision the user experience on your library website?

A: Our vision for the user experience is centered on simplicity and efficiency. We aim to create an intuitive interface where users can easily navigate through the catalog, find relevant information, and engage with interactive features. Incorporating user feedback is crucial to refining the website, ensuring it meets the evolving needs of our diverse user base.
